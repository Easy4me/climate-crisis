The Palisades Fire

Los Angeles, CA
Feb 2, 2025

Finally, after 24 of days of burning, the Palisades Fire is 100% contained as of January 31. The burn area ends at 23,707 acres (about 37 square miles)—one of our smaller wildfires, but 6,837 buildings were destroyed or damaged—making this fire the 3rd most damaging in CA history. Despite evacuation efforts, 12 are reported dead, marking it as the 10th deadliest in our state. This serves as a harsh reminder of the real dangers of wildfires.

What can civilians do?

Individuals can take action to prevent fires and stay safe during fire season:

  • Follow burn bans and fire warnings
  • Prepare an evacuation plan
  • Stay informed via local alerts
